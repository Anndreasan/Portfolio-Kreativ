STHLM Fasion week - Past Tense

During Stockholm Fashion Week, I worked as a lighting technician with the clothing brand Past Tense.

My role was to design and operate lighting that highlighted the collection and enhanced the overall atmosphere of the show.

By adapting the lighting to the garments, movement, and music, I helped create a cohesive and visually impactful experience where fashion and stage design came together.
